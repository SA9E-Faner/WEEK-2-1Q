What is Unordered List?

  • It is a list of related items without order
  • By default, the items are listed with bullets

What is Ordered List?

  1. It is a list of related items with order
  2. By default, the items are listed with Hindu-Arabic numerals

Example of Nested list

  1. Philippine Animals
    • Tamaraw
    • Pilandok
    • Tarsier
    • Haribon
  2. Philippine Fruits
    • Mango
    • Atis
    • Avocado
    • Coconut
    • Banana
